BIOGRAPHY

Hello! My name is Eric Syme and I am 37 years old. This past year my wife and I were blessed with our first child; our daughter, Ruby. This comes on the heels of purchasing our first home as well, so you could say the past few years have been eventful! I love spending quality time with my family in the great outdoors. When my wife and I first met, we bonded over many hikes and backpacking trips in the North Cascades. I myself dabble in mountaineering as well and have summited Mt. Adams with a few friends in 2012. I love to snowboard, fish and explore; I suppose I’m a kid a heart! I caught my first Steelhead salmon on the Skykomish River a few years ago and now I'm 'hooked'!!! My wife and I have been on many fishing trips together locally. She always has the knack of catching the first fish. Every time. One thing that I love about living in Kitsap County is that we’re so close to any number of outdoor activities we want to do. I am SO excited to pass on my passions to my daughter.

I am passionate about music and the arts in general, and my favorite thing to do on a rainy day is kick back with my family and listen to records in our play room. I love to cook too! Put me in any kitchen and I’m home.

What I’m excited about recently is being able to start a new career (my first true career) as a web developer. I’m very curious by nature and learning how to code and create webpages has been monumental for me. I can’t look at a webpage now without wondering how it was created and wanting to take it apart to figure out how it ticks. I’ll be full-stack certified by the middle of August, but I can tell this is only the beginning of my relationship of understanding and growing with code.
